Scan a heap region for HIST_ENTRY structs.

HIST_ENTRY layout (24 bytes on 64-bit): offset 0: char *line (pointer to command string) offset 8: char *timestamp (pointer to “#DIGITS” string, or NULL) offset 16: histdata_t *data (usually NULL)
